Before we start

This isn't a "look at your grades and feel bad" activity. This is a systems check — like a mechanic running diagnostics. Even a car that's running fine has things to tune up.

The deal: Be brutally honest. No one is grading this for correctness. If you lie to this tool, you're only cheating yourself out of useful information. The students who get the most out of this are the ones who stop performing "good student" and start being real.
